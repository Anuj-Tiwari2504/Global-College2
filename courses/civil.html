Program Overview

Our Civil Engineering program focuses on infrastructure development, construction management, and sustainable design practices. Students learn to design, build, and maintain the physical and naturally built environment including roads, bridges, buildings, and water systems.

Career Opportunities

  • Structural Engineer - Design buildings, bridges, and infrastructure
  • Construction Manager - Oversee construction projects and teams
  • Project Engineer - Manage engineering projects from conception to completion
  • Urban Planner - Design and plan urban development projects
  • Infrastructure Consultant - Provide expert advice on infrastructure projects
  • Site Engineer - Supervise construction activities on-site
  • Quality Control Engineer - Ensure construction quality and standards
